1. Introduction

Finans & CRM Pro ("the App") is a financial management and CRM application designed for small businesses, entrepreneurs, and freelancers in Turkey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • User profile information (business type, company name)

Financial Data You Enter

  • Credit card tracking data (card names, statement dates, payment amounts)
  • Check and promissory note records
  • POS transaction data and Z-reports
  • Income and expense records
  • Tax calendar reminders
  • Family wallet transactions

CRM Data

  • Contacts, companies, and deals you create
  • Activities, notes, and communications
  • Custom fields and tags

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information (device type, OS version) for app compatibility
  • App usage analytics (crash reports, performance metrics via Firebase)

3. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure:

  • Firebase Authentication: Handles user login securely with industry-standard encryption
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your financial and CRM data in Google's encrypted cloud database
  • Firebase Cloud Storage: Stores uploaded files and documents

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is encrypted using Google's default encryption.

The App also supports offline mode using IndexedDB on your device. Offline data is synchronized with the cloud when connectivity is restored.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core financial management and CRM features
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To generate reports and analytics within the App
  • To send tax deadline reminders and payment notifications
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your data may be shared only with:

  • Google Firebase: As our infrastructure provider (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Accountant Portal: If you create a share link, your designated accountant can view read-only financial data through a time-limited, token-based link. You control this sharing.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs. The App contains no ads.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through Settings, all your data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and KVKK (Turkish Personal Data Protection Law), you have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (account deletion available in Settings)
  • Export your data (PDF, Excel, CSV export available in the App)
  • Opt-out of analytics data collection
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Finans & CRM Pro is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, we will promptly delete it.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Firebase Authentication with secure token management
  • Firestore security rules limiting data access to authenticated users
  • No storage of plain-text passwords
  • KVKK compliance monitoring and audit logging

10. Subscriptions & Payments

Subscription payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment card information. Payment processing is handled entirely by the respective platform. See our Terms of Use for subscription details.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
